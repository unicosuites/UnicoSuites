Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to remember preferences and collect analytics about how the site is used. Some cookies are essential to making a site work; others are optional and require your consent under UK GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

Cookies we use on this site

We use a deliberately small number of cookies, in two categories:

Essential cookies (always on)

  • us-cookie-consent-v1 — stored in localStorage on your device to remember your cookie preference so we don't ask again. Expires when you clear browser storage.

Essential cookies cannot be switched off because they are necessary for the site to function and to remember your consent choice.

Analytics cookies (opt-in only)

  • _ga, _ga_* — set by Google Analytics 4 (GA4) only after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. These cookies measure aggregated, anonymous visitor behaviour: pages viewed, time on page, device type. IP addresses are anonymised before processing. Cookies expire after 14 months.

Analytics cookies help us understand which pages are most useful and where the site could be improved. They are completely optional — declining them has no effect on your ability to use the site.

How to manage your preferences

You can change your cookie choice at any time:

  • On this site: clear your browser's localStorage for unicosuites.com — the cookie banner will reappear on your next visit and you can choose again.
  • In your browser: all modern browsers let you block or delete cookies from settings — see aboutcookies.org for browser-specific instructions.
  • Block Google Analytics: install the official Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Third-party cookies on linked services

When you click through to external services (Airbnb, Booking.com, Google Maps, our social profiles), those services set their own cookies under their own privacy policies. We don't control those cookies — please review the relevant provider's policy.

Related policies

For more on how we handle personal data generally, see our privacy policy. If you have questions about this cookie policy, email hello@unicosuites.com.
