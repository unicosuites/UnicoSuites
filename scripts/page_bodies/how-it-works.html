The process

How Unico Suites works.

From free property review to first booking — usually in under a week. Here's exactly what happens, and exactly what's included once we take the keys.

Four steps

A clear path from enquiry to first booking.

  1. 1

    Free property review

    A 20-minute conversation about the property, your goals and your numbers. We answer your questions, talk through pricing, and confirm whether the property is a good fit for serviced accommodation.

  2. 2

    On-site assessment

    We visit the property to value it as a short-let, scope any minor improvements (smart lock, white goods, soft-furnishings), and put a projected monthly income figure in front of you in writing.

  3. 3

    Listing & setup

    Professional photography, listing copy, channel onboarding (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, direct), smart-lock fit-out, hotel-grade linen, consumables, welcome pack — typically inside seven days.

  4. 4

    We manage everything

    Bookings, guests, cleaning, maintenance, reporting — handled in-house. You receive a monthly statement with full breakdown and a deposit straight into your account.

Guaranteed rent option

Prefer a fixed monthly figure? We can do that too.

For landlords who prioritise predictability over upside, we offer a guaranteed-rent model on selected properties — you receive a fixed monthly payment, on the same day each month, regardless of occupancy. We absorb the void risk and keep the management margin.

Guaranteed rent typically pays a notch below the projected managed-SA net, in exchange for the certainty. Whether it's the right model for you depends on the property and your risk appetite — something we'll talk through honestly at your free property review.
