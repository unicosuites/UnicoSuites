The process

How Unico Suites works.

From free property review to first booking — usually in under a week. Here's exactly what happens, and exactly what's included once we take the keys.

Four steps

A clear path from enquiry to first booking.

  1. 1

    Free property review

    A 20-minute conversation about the property, your goals and your numbers. We answer your questions, talk through pricing, and confirm whether the property is a good fit for serviced accommodation.

  2. 2

    On-site assessment

    We visit the property to value it as a short-let, scope any minor improvements (white goods, soft-furnishings, finishing touches), and put a projected monthly income figure in front of you in writing.

  3. 3

    Listing & setup

    Professional photography, listing copy, channel onboarding (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, direct), staging, hotel-grade linen, consumables, welcome pack.

  4. 4

    We manage everything

    Bookings, guests, cleaning, maintenance, reporting — handled in-house. You receive a monthly statement with full breakdown and a payout straight into your account.
