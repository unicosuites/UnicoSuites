About Unico Suites

Local Bedford operators, backed by decades of estate-agency, lettings and property-investment experience — running one of the region's premium serviced accommodation portfolios.

Our story

Built on the shoulders of a 1,000+ property group.

Unico Suites was founded to bring institutional-grade serviced accommodation management to landlords in Bedford and the wider region — without losing the personal, local touch that makes a property easy to live with.

We sit inside an established estate-agency and lettings group that has been managing residential property across Bedfordshire, Northamptonshire and Hertfordshire for decades. With over a thousand active managed properties in the group, we have the operational scale to deliver hotel-grade service — and the local relationships to make every property feel like it was set up by hand.

For landlords, that means a hands-off experience with reliable returns. For guests, that means a stay that consistently outperforms the rest of the market.

Why Bedford

A market with real demand drivers.

Bedford sits at the centre of one of the UK's strongest growth corridors: 40 minutes to London by train, immediate access to the M1, and minutes from Milton Keynes, Luton and Cambridge.

Short-stay demand is driven by a deep mix: contractors on long-cycle infrastructure projects (the East-West rail link, Wixams expansion, A421 widening), business travellers connecting through Luton and Milton Keynes, NHS visitors to Bedford Hospital, and families relocating into the area.

Bedford's growth runway is just beginning. The Universal Studios UK theme park announced for nearby Kempston / Stewartby is projected to draw millions of visitors a year once open — a transformation that's already reshaping the local short-stay market and the wider economy.

For landlords, that means a market with structural undersupply, sustained demand and a clear case for premium serviced accommodation over traditional letting.
