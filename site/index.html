Who we are

Expert property management, simplified.

Unico Suites is the serviced accommodation arm of an established group managing over 1,000 properties across Bedfordshire, Northamptonshire and Hertfordshire. We turn long-let homes into premium short-stay assets — fully hands-off, fully accountable.

  • Higher income than a traditional AST tenancy in most areas we operate in.
  • Zero day-to-day — bookings, cleaning, linen, guests, maintenance — all handled in-house.
  • Transparent reporting with monthly statements and live occupancy dashboards.
  • Local expertise in Bedford from a founding team that lives and lets in the market.

What we do

A complete management service.

Every part of running a serviced accommodation — handled in-house, end-to-end, with the same attention to detail you'd give it yourself.

Guest communication

Vetted, vouched-for guests with full ID checks. We handle every message, request and review — 24/7, in your house style.

Dynamic pricing

Real-time pricing tuned to local events, demand and seasonality so we capture the highest rate without sitting empty.

Multi-platform listing

Professionally listed and synced across Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo and direct — channel-managed to avoid double bookings.

Cleaning & linen

Hotel-standard turnaround, fresh hotel-grade linen and consumables restocked between every stay — coordinated by our in-house team.

Maintenance & inspections

Routine inspections plus a vetted contractor network. Issues spotted early, fixed before they become problems — at trade rates.

Monthly reporting

Transparent monthly statement and dashboard — occupancy, nightly rate, opex and net to you. No surprises, no spreadsheets.

The process

From key handover to first booking in days.

  1. 1

    Free property review

    A 20-minute call to understand your goals, the property and your numbers. No obligation.

  2. 2

    Property assessment

    An in-person visit to value the home as a short-let, agree any small upgrades and confirm projected returns.

  3. 3

    Listing & setup

    Professional photography, copywriting, channel listing, smart-lock fit-out, linen, consumables — done in under a week.

  4. 4

    We manage everything

    Bookings, guests, cleaning, maintenance, reporting — handled. You receive a monthly statement and a deposit into your account.
